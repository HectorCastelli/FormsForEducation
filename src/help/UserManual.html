Resending an application email

If a student doesn't receive an email and you want to resent that student's test to a different address, you have two options:

  1. Use the Resend Application option on the menu;
  2. Manually send the email with the data from the Professor email

For option 2, you can copy the section of the email relevant to the student and paste it into a new email.
